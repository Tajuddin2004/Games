Genshin Impact
Genshin Impact is an open-world action RPG by miHoYo. Explore the magical land of Teyvat, discover elemental powers, complete quests, and assemble your team of heroes in a beautiful, anime-inspired adventure with regular updates and deep lore.
Game Specs
  • Genre: Open World, Action RPG
  • Multiplayer: Co-op up to 4 players
  • Developer: miHoYo (HoYoverse)
  • Size: ~35GB+ (PC), Varies on mobile
  • Price: Free-to-Play (with in-app purchases)
System Requirements
Minimum (Windows):
  • OS: Windows 7 SP1 64-bit, Windows 8.1 64-bit or Windows 10 64-bit
  • CPU: Intel Core i5 equivalent or higher
  • RAM: 8GB
  • GPU: NVIDIA GeForce GT 1030 or higher
  • Storage: 35GB available space
Recommended (Windows):
  • CPU: Intel Core i7 equivalent or higher
  • RAM: 16GB
  • GPU: NVIDIA GeForce GTX 1060 6GB or higher
  • Storage: 35GB+ SSD
Mobile (Android/iOS):
  • Android: ARM v8a 64-bit devices, 4GB+ RAM, Android 7.0+
  • iOS: iPhone 8 Plus, iOS 12.0+, 8GB+ free storage
